Spawner

I made this spawner which in the end placed everything at the spawners location.
But it had a lot of settings. The spawner consist of two parts the spawner and the spawner manager.

The spawner manager has a tag and class to search for to find all spawners in the level a list of what to give to each spawner

The spawner has setable roof level to control from how high it will draw the line traces, floor level where thr floor is, floor buffer how far below the floor level it should check and props min z for the minumum height props can spawn and radius how far away from the spawner things can spawn.
Assets are received from the Spawn Manager the settings under Advanced are shown wurther down.
