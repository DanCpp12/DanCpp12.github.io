Description

TheRacingDrift III is my graduation project for FutureGames. It is a racing game with my own custom wheels, suspension and engine systems.

Engine

The engine is a simple power curve based on the Nissan GTR where the X-axis is the RPM and the Y-axis is the horsepower.
I got the idea to make a turbo but after making it and not noticing a difference in the car's acceleration thought of making the engine with cylinders and pistons. But after asking our mentor who said that engines are usually just a power curve and I also realised that it would take too much time. So not noticing a difference I scrapped the turbo entirely.

Transmission

The transmission takes the current gear's gear ratio from the Array then multiplies it with the final drive ratio to get the effective ratio.
The effective ratio is then used to calculate the output torque along the evaluated engine curve and throttle.

Suspension

the suspension coinsists of two parts the normal suspension wich is just a spring force and a dampening force but then there is the anti-sway bar witch in real life is a rorsion spring connecting left and right side suspension. but in the game it is a lerp conecting the two springs but because of a small error on my part when adding it it dose not get mentioned in the report as i solved it after the turnin of the report.

Wheels

The wheels were the most dificult to get right mainly because i wanted them to roll corectly. as i wanted the wheels to roll with the speed of the car and the engine.

Then there is the friction this can make or break the feel of the car. here i feel like i succeded because the wheels feels stable and controlable when you want them to be but when you want to drift they letgo and let you in to a controled drift.
